DOMException: Codec reclaimed due to inactivity.

Hardware codecs should only be reclaimed if too many are allocated (the exact # depends varies per platform), and the codec is inactive, and the page is in the background.
硬件编解码器只有在分配的数量过多（具体数量因平台而异）、编解码器处于非活动状态且页面处于后台的情况下才会被回收。
